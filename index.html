About

Kifuliiru Lab is a research subsidiary of Wekify LLC developing scalable methodologies for indigenous language documentation and preservation. We combine computational linguistics, template-based generation, and community engagement to create comprehensive educational resources for under-resourced languages.

Our work demonstrates that systematic content generation, when paired with native speaker validation, can produce educational materials at unprecedented scale—transforming languages from minimal documentation to comprehensive digital ecosystems.

141,000+ Books Generated
8GB Training Data
100% Community Validated
3 Years Zero to Complete

Research Focus

Primary Question: Can template-based computational generation produce authentic, pedagogically-sound educational content for languages with minimal existing written materials?

Methodology: Dual-track approach combining (1) systematic generation via mathematical formulas and linguistic templates, and (2) community-powered content creation through digital platforms.

Validation: All generated content undergoes multi-stage community review including native speaker verification and elder cultural validation.

Hypothesis: This methodology is language-agnostic and can scale to the 3,000+ endangered languages globally.

Active Projects

  • Number Generation System Complete

    Mathematical formulas encoding Kifuliiru number rules. Generates any number (1→∞) with 100% accuracy. 8GB training data produced. Proof of concept for template-based methodology. 10,000+ books

  • Mathematics Question Generator In Progress

    Automated generation of culturally-contextualized math problems. All operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division). Uses community-contributed vocabulary for authentic scenarios. 40,000+ books targeted

  • Verb Conjugation System In Progress

    Systematic documentation of 250+ verbs across all tenses and persons. Template-based sentence generation combining verbs with community-validated nouns and proper names. 30,000+ books | 119M+ possible sentences

  • Community Publishing Platforms Operational

    Digital infrastructure for native content creation: Imyazi (news), Tuyandike (creative writing), Blog (commentary), Radio Ibufuliiru (audio). 50+ active contributors. All content archived for book compilation. 11,000+ books | Growing daily

  • AI Language Model Training Planned Q1 2026

    Fine-tuning large language models on Kifuliiru corpus. Applications: conversational chatbot, translation assistance, grammar checking, cultural knowledge base. First comprehensive AI for Kifuliiru language.
